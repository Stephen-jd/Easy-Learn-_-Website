How It Works

Simple, structured, and fully online — from enrollment to completion

1
College Partners with Us
The college signs up with Easy Learn Academy to run a technical bootcamp program for their students online.
2
Trainer is Assigned
An expert corporate trainer is assigned and approved by our admin team to deliver structured daily online sessions.
3
Live Online Sessions
Students attend 2–3 live online sessions per day. Trainers log daily progress, topics covered, and upload class notes.
4
Certificate & Completion
Students complete assignments and receive a course completion certificate verified by Easy Learn Academy.
